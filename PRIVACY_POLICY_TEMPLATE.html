Privacy Policy for TechFirstSearch

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Summary: We do NOT collect any personal information. This app is completely private and doesn't track you in any way.

Overview

TechFirstSearch ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices for the iOS mobile application.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No email addresses
  • No names or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers (IDFA, UDID, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No camera or photo access
  • No microphone access
  • No contacts access
  • No health data
  • No payment information

What the App Does

TechFirstSearch is a content aggregator that:

  • Displays tech content: We fetch and display articles, papers, and news from public sources (TechCrunch, ArXiv, Medium, GitHub, etc.)
  • Requires internet: The app needs internet connection to load content from our servers
  • No user tracking: We don't know who you are, what you read, or how you use the app

Local Device Storage

The app may store data locally on your device for functionality:

  • Recently viewed articles (cached for faster loading)
  • App preferences (theme, settings)
  • Search history (if implemented, stored locally only)

Important: This data:

  • Stays on your device only
  • Is never sent to our servers
  • Is never associated with your identity
  • Is completely deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Content

We aggregate content from external sources including but not limited to:

  • Hacker News, Dev.to, TechCrunch, ArXiv, Medium, GitHub, and others

When you click links to read original articles:

  • You may be directed to third-party websites
  • Those websites have their own privacy policies
  • We have no control over their data practices
  • We recommend reviewing their privacy policies

No Third-Party Analytics or Tracking

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook SDK
  • Advertising networks
  • Any tracking or analytics services

Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we don't collect any data.

We have nothing to share with:

  • Third-party companies
  • Advertisers
  • Data brokers
  • Anyone

Children's Privacy

Our app does not target children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us (though we don't collect such information).

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no personal data to secure. However:

  • We use HTTPS for all network communications
  • Content is fetched securely from our servers
  • Local device storage uses iOS secure storage mechanisms

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there's no data to:

  • Access
  • Correct
  • Delete
  • Export
  • Restrict processing

You are already in complete control of any data stored locally on your device.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information. Since we do not collect any personal information, these rights do not apply to our app.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provides you with certain rights. Since we do not collect or process personal data, GDPR obligations do not apply to our app.
